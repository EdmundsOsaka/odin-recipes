Pulled Pork

Description

A simple and tasty pulled pork recipe made with the flavoring of Dr Pepper.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of Dr. Pepper
  • 2 cups of BBQ sauce
  • 1 pound of pork tenderloin
  • 4 hamburger buns

Method

  1. Combine soda and BBQ sauce in a pressure cooker. Add pork tenderloin. Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure. Set timer for 50 minutes.
  2. Release pressure using the naural-release method (10 -> 40 minutes)
  3. Remove pork loin and drain fluid from pot. Shred pork using 2 forks. Return to the pot and add extra BBQ sauec.
  4. Serve on hamburger buns.

Kung Pao Chicken

Description

A stir-fried Chinese dish made with chicken, peanuts and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • White Wine
  • Sesame Oil
  • Skinless, Boneless Chicken
  • Hot Chile Paste
  • Brown Sugar
  • White Vinegar
  • Chopped Peanuts
  • Chopped Garlic and Onions

Method

  1. Combine water and cornstarch in a cup. Set aside.
  2. Combine wine, soy sauce, sesame oil and cornstarch/water mixture in a large glass bowl. Add chicken pieces and toss to coat. Cover dish and refridgerate for about 30 minutes.
  3. Whisk in chile paste, brown sugar and vinegar. Add peanuts, onions and garlic.
  4. Remove chicken from marinade. Add to large skillet and cook over medium heat.
  5. Adjust heat and simmer together until sauce thickens.
